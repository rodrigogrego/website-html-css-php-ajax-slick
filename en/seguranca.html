How we handle security at LM:


The changes in the corporate world, generated by technological innovations and organizations, have significantly increased activities in companies, facilitating or eliminating more difficult or cumbersome tasks. This relationship between man and technology has produced new risks for the safety and health of workers, both in terms of physical, mental or social. These changes started to demand a higher qualification and a increasing intervention in production processes, the which consequently make them more susceptible to accidents at work

Given this reality, and with continuous investment in raising the awareness of its employees, employment of safe practices, use of technology and continuous promotion of security, LM Engenharia is proud of its commitment to the prevention of occupational injuries and illnesses. When we take care of the safety and health of our employees, we are also taking care of all involved in our operations.

LM Engenharia's occupational health and safety management system provides the necessary confidence for its customers, aware that the services offered add quality, price and, above all, the safety of everyone involved.
