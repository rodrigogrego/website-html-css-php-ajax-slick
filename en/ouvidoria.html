Welcome to LM Engenharia's ethics line communication channel!


This is the open channel made available by the company that aims to facilitate the contribution of Customers, Suppliers and Third Parties through communication. In this channel you can present compliments, suggestions, complaints and denunciations that will collaborate with the sustainability of an increasingly secure and ethical corporate environment. LM Engenharia is committed to, according to its policy, promoting transparent communication between the levels of the organization and with the other interested parties. The information recorded here will be analyzed and treated, if desired, anonymously, if desired, and stratified in order to improve internal aspects and service to stakeholders.
