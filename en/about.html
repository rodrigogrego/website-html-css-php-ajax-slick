SERVICES

With a culture centered on customer needs, LM Engineering acts multidisciplinarly in the industrial area, throughout the national territory, with its operations based on principles more modern in terms of security and technology requirements, using formidable market experience to take care of your facilities and positively impact your company, aiming at your better productivity.

We have trained and specialized labor to perform services in the sectors of Electromechanical, Mechanical, Electrical and Civil in the petrochemical, oil and gas areas, as well as manufacture and assembly of metallic structures, pipes and boilers, being able to perform:


  • Structuring of planned maintenance for new operating units;

  • Integral management of industrial plant maintenance;

  • Planning and execution of mechanical, boiler, electrical, instrumentation, automation, civil and complementary services;

  • Offshore construction, assembly and maintenance;

  • Technical painting and structural integrity;

  • Industrial cleaning.
