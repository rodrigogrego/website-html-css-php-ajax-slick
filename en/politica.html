LM Engenharia has the following commitments and priorities in its management of quality, environmental and safety and health:


  • Seek customer satisfaction in all its projects;

  • Acting in accordance with the principles established in its mission, vision and values;

  • Meet the needs and expectations of customers and other interested parties;

  • Continuously improve processes;

  • Contribute to the protection of the environment and prevention of pollution wherever its services are performed, eliminating or reducing the environmental impacts arising from its activities, such as pollutant emissions and waste disposal;

  • Work to prevent, eliminate or minimize the health and safety risks generated in the development of its activities and to which its employees are exposed;

  • Comply with applicable legislation and other subscribed requirements.
